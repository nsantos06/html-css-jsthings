What Is Computer Science?

Computer science is the study of processes that interact with data and that can be represented as data in the form of programs.
It enables the use of algorithms to manipulate, store, and communicate digital information.
A computer scientist studies the theory of computation and the design of software systems.

What Is Algorithms?

In mathematics and computer science, an algorithm is a finite sequence of well-defined, computer-implementable instructions.
Typically to solve a class of problems or to perform a computation.
Algorithms are always unambiguous and are used as specifications for performing calculations, data processing, automated reasoning, and other tasks.
